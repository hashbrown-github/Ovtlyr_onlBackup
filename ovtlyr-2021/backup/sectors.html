Fear & Greed Meter (Nov 22, 2021)

What is this chart?

The Fear & Greed Meter reflects investor behaviors relative to market rationality. Stocks in red have been fearfully oversold, whereas those in blue are irrationally overvalued. Each sector considers all constituents covered by OVTLYR to create even & cap weighted overall scores.

Combine Chart

Heatmap & Predicted Trajectory

Heatmap shows the condition of shareholder fear/greed for the stock. Extreme red & blue values represent the most fearful or greedy states respectively.

Trajectory represents a modeled prediction of price action for the current trading day (values updated prior to market open).

Correlation

Correlation values are calculated on a rolling short-term period directly from price action.
