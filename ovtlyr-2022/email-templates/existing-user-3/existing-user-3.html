Here's a brief overview of how OVTLYR works and why you should consider incorporating it in your investing journey:

Equity markets are driven by human emotions – more specifically, how we act and react to those emotions. This collective action is Investor Behavior. Frequently, those decisions are clouded by one or more cognitive biases which impart the noise of irrationality upon markets assumed to be populated exclusively by rational participants.

OVTLYR measures this “noise,” compares daily market activity against a baseline of fundamental & macroeconomic data, and summarizes overly negative or positive behaviors as fear or greed, respectively.

Always remember that the stock market is an auction where buyers and sellers decide on price & trajectory through a process of reaching equilibrium. Imagine an auditorium full of people participating in a daily poll for XYZ stock, deciding who’s bullish or bearish and by how much. Now, suppose the audience is overwhelmingly bullish with few remaining on the bearish side. Eventually, there will be no additional market participants willing to conform to the popular opinion and become buyers rather than sellers of XYZ… no new buyers, no more price appreciation.

This is the heart of OVTLYR’s proprietary allocation and portfolio engines, leveraging a combination of our investor behavioral data, intelligent machines with perfect memory recall, and the concept of “selective contrarianism” applied to over 1600 US-listed stocks (with a cumulative market cap north of $50T) and 150 indexed EFTs across all 11 GICS sectors and their corresponding industries. This systematic analysis of information enables us to predict stock price trajectory, creating windows of opportunities to benefit from it.
